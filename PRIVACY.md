# Privacy Policy — Still

**Last updated: March 24, 2026**

Still is a Safari extension that blocks animated images and motion effects in your browser.

## Data Collection

Still does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. The extension runs entirely on your device.

## What the Extension Accesses

Still reads web page content solely to detect and block animated images (GIF, WebP, APNG), autoplay videos, CSS transitions, and SVG animations. This processing happens locally on your device and no page content is sent to any server.

## Storage

Still uses your browser's local storage to save your preferences (enabled/disabled state and per-site allowlist). This data never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

Still does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
