Race-condition fixture: lazy-inserted AR videos

Reproduces the race that real Google Shopping hit: a card scrolls into view (or you hover), Google's JS inserts a <video> element AND synchronously calls .play() on it in the same tick — before the extension's MutationObserver has a chance to tag it. The earlier defense (gated only on the data-still-video attribute) leaked here. The race-safe defense (gates on the src URL too, synchronously inside the play() override) catches it.
