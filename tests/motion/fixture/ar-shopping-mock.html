Mock Google Shopping AR-spin carousel

Fixture for the Still extension. The four videos below are the real mp4s that Google embeds in Shopping carousels (paths captured from prior diagnostic runs against live SERPs). Each <video> has the exact attribute set Google ships (no autoplay attribute, but muted+playsinline+aria-hidden+ disableremoteplayback+preload="none"). The script below calls play() on every video after a 1.5 s delay, simulating the IntersectionObserver/hover trigger that ordinary autoplay blockers can't catch.
